## Description

Forget scrolling through endless search results. SoGoJet turns flight discovery into something you actually enjoy — swipe through live flight deals the way you scroll your favorite feed.

**HOW IT WORKS**

Swipe right on deals you love. Swipe left to skip. It takes two seconds to know if a fare is worth your time. No filters to wrestle with, no calendar grids to decode. Just real prices on real flights, one card at a time.

**LIVE PRICES. NOT LAST WEEK'S PRICES.**

Every deal you see is pulled live from Duffel, one of the world's most trusted flight data providers. The price on your screen is the price you pay. No bait-and-switch. No "fees revealed at checkout." What you swipe is what you get.

**272+ DESTINATIONS AND COUNTING**

From weekend escapes to bucket-list adventures, SoGoJet covers routes across major airlines and regional carriers worldwide. New destinations are added regularly — there is always somewhere new to discover.

**SAVE, COMPARE, THEN BOOK**

Found a deal you like but not ready to commit? Save it. Stack up your favorites side by side. When you are ready, book directly inside the app with secure Stripe-powered payments. No redirects, no third-party checkout pages, no surprises.

**THE EXPERIENCE**

SoGoJet is designed around a vintage jet-age airline terminal aesthetic — the golden era of travel, reimagined for your phone. Departure boards, retro typography, warm tones. It feels like stepping into an airport lounge from 1965, except the prices are from 2026.

**KEY FEATURES**

- TikTok-style swipe interface for browsing flight deals
- Live pricing powered by Duffel — always accurate, always current
- 272+ destinations across major and regional airlines
- Save and compare your favorite deals before booking
- Book and pay directly in-app with Stripe
- Vintage airline terminal design that stands apart from every other travel app
- No hidden fees — the price you see is the price you pay

**WHO IT'S FOR**

Spontaneous travelers. Deal hunters. Anyone tired of the same stale travel app experience. If you have ever wished finding a flight was as easy as swiping through a feed — this is your app.

Download SoGoJet. Swipe through live deals. Book the one that makes you grab your passport.
